About the Projects

This final project is the culmination of the skills students have developed throughout the semester in the CSCI 4830/6830 Virtual Reality course. Working in teams, students were tasked to design, develop, and demonstrate a collaborative Mixed Reality (MR) or Virtual Reality (VR) application.

The goal for each team was to create a functional and polished experience designed to teach or practice a useful skill. These applications go beyond pure entertainment; they represent complex technical achievements that meet rigorous requirements:

  • Collaborative Multi-User Experience: Multiple users interact simultaneously within a shared virtual environment.
  • AI-Driven Characters: Each scene features intelligent NPCs that users interact with via voice, gestures, or actions.
  • Advanced Interaction: Projects utilize intuitive UI systems, custom avatars for VR presence, or spatial anchors for persistent Mixed Reality alignment.
